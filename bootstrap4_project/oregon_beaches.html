About the Oregon Coast

Oregon beaches are located between Washington beaches and California beaches.

They're also located between Oregon and the Pacific Ocean.

Many whales, sea lions, starfish, and otter animals call this region home.

It's only summer for a few days a year, so make sure you bundle up!

Things to do:

  • Walk on the Beach
  • Bike along the Shore
  • Deep Sea Fishing
  • Wildlife Watching
